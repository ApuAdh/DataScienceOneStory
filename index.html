In Pokémon, natures influence a Pokémon's stat growth by increasing one stat while decreasing another

In Pokémon, each Pokémon has six main stats: HP (Hit Points), Attack, Defense, Special Attack, Special Defense, and Speed

These stats determine a Pokémon's performance in battles. The base stats are inherent

to each species, while individual values (IVs) and effort values (EVs) add a layer of individuality and customization.

Trainers strategically train and breed Pokémon to optimize these stats for competitive battles
