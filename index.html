Looking at the graph you might think that Arceus absolutely dogwater Infernape. But there is one crucial detail we are missing. Well that might be a little confusing so let's slow down a bit. In Pokemon there isn't a one "Strongest Pokemon". There are many multiple things that can have an effect on a battle. In the previous paragraph we talked about pokemon types and held items. There are multiple different held items that exist in pokemon. They all have a cool effect on the battlefield, the Choice Scarf locks your pokemon to be able to only use one move but increases the speed stat by 1.5X

Altough held items play a crucial role in pokemon battles, the biggest thing that affects a fight between two pokemon is their type.
